2. **Sign up** (if needed) or **Sign in**

2. **Click "Sign Up"** → **"Continue with GitHub"**
3. **Authorize Vercel** (click allow)
4. **Click "New Project"**
5. **Find your repo** → **Click "Import"**
6. **Just click "Deploy"** (Vercel auto-detects React)
